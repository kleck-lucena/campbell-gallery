Seanwyn's Creations Gallery

"I am a Welsh Artist that specialises in Biro-pen illustrations.
The term Weirdly Beautiful perfectly describes my little monsters that I have conjured up.
They are simultaneously quirky and grotesque and have all spawned from the depths of my imagination.
They are drawn in my unusual style and humour via character poses often with weird, often plant-like extremities. I hope you like them."
